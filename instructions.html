How iRocket Confidence Works

1. Take your confidence quiz

Start with a quick assessment to pinpoint exactly where to focus your growth-whether it's self-
advocacy, public speaking, or owning your achievements.

2. Get your personalized plan

We'll generate a custom calendar packed with daily confidence challenges tailored just for you. Each
task is designed to stretch your comfort zone in manageable steps.

3. Earn Points & Build Momentum

Small steps = Big wins: Complete individual steps to rack up points.

Bonus boost: Finish the full task for extra rewards.

Streak rewards Consecutive days of completed tasks? That's a streak! Keep it alive to unlock achievements.

4. Rise or Reset

Keep climbing: Every full task you complete extends your streak.

No judgement, just progress: Miss a day? Your streak resets, but your progress never disappears.
Tomorrow is a fresh start.

Why It Works: Tiny, consistent actions rewire your brain for confidence. Think of it like a workout, but
for your courage.
